Sreedhareeyam Eye Hospital

Redefining Ayurveda

Welcome to Sreedhareeyam Ayurvedic Eye Hospital & Research centre, a beacon of hope for those seeking natural and holistic eye care solutions. Nestled in the heart of serene greenery at Koothattukulam, in the Indian state of Kerala, Sreedhareeyam is a renowned institution dedicated to preserving and restoring the precious gift of sight through the time-tested wisdom of Ayurveda. With over three decades of unwavering commitment to excellence, we have earned a reputation as a world-class destination for those in pursuit of unparalleled eye care.

At Sreedhareeyam, we combine the ancient principles of Ayurveda with cutting-edge technology to provide comprehensive eye care services that cater to a wide spectrum of eye conditions and other ailments. Our team of highly skilled Ayurvedic physicians, ophthalmologists, and support staff work in harmony to offer personalized treatment plans tailored to each individual's unique needs. Patients from all corners of the globe entrust their health to us, seeking relief from common eye ailments or exploring alternatives to invasive surgeries. Discover and experience the transformative power of Ayurveda!

Sreedhareeyam Campus

Nestled amidst the lush greenery of Kerala, India, Sreedhareeyam's campus is a serene haven where nature's healing powers come to life. Our tranquil environment harmonizes with Ayurvedic wisdom to offer a natural sanctuary for holistic vision care and rejuvenation. Here, we understand that healing involves not only the body but also the mind, fostering a sense of inner peace and well-being.
