Ayurvedic Ophthalmology

Shalakya Tantra is a branch of Ayurveda dedicated to addressing ailments above the neck. Shalakya Tantra offers specific procedures for neck and head organs, such as Nasyam, Dhoomapanam, Gandoosham, and Shirovasthi, proving highly effective for various diseases, including degenerative and infectious conditions. Ayurvedic texts like Sushrut Samhita and Charak Samhita provide comprehensive insights into Shalakya Tantra, covering the causes, diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of head and neck diseases. These texts list around 76 eye diseases, 25 ear conditions, 18 nose issues, and 11 lip ailments, all of which Shalakya Tantra addresses thoroughly. It not only focuses on immediate cures but also ensures complete eradication of diseases through follow-up treatments.

Sreedhareeyam Eye Hospital

Sreedhareeyam, a leading center for the treatment of eye diseases, offers comprehensive care for all 72 eye ailments mentioned in Ayurveda, including those affecting the cornea, eyelids, optic nerves, and retina. The clinic's doctors combine professional qualifications with traditional knowledge passed down through generations. Traditional methods are employed to diagnose ailments, complemented by modern diagnostic tools. This balanced approach reduces reliance on a single system, resulting in unmatched treatment outcomes. The treatments undergo continuous research and clinical evaluation, ensuring success in treating both common and rare diseases.
