Message from Vice Chairman

Sreedhareeyam has always upheld traditional values in the pursuit of improving healthcare for the future. Ayurveda, a timeless science developed by ancient sages like Charaka, Susrutha, and Vaghbhada, stands as the legacy of dedicated researchers who dedicated their lives to addressing human ailments using nature's resources. Ayurveda encompasses various specialized fields documented in ancient texts. Centuries ago, each Indian village had distinct families specializing in different aspects of Ayurvedic care. Sreedhareeyam proudly traces its roots to the Nellikattu Mana, a family with centuries of expertise in Ayurvedic Ophthalmology, firmly rooted in preserving ancestral values. We eagerly anticipate the convergence of modern healthcare technology and Ayurvedic medical science. From its inception, Sreedhareeyam has integrated advanced technology for diagnosis and documentation, heralding a new era in Ayurvedic healthcare.

Ayurveda, a longstanding science, has enriched lives throughout history and continues to offer natural healthcare solutions to millions today. We are confident that Ayurveda will lead to a healthier, more natural future for countless individuals. Sreedhareeyam remains devoted to the cause of Ayurveda, striving to deliver its very best.
