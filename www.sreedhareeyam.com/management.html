Message from Founder

At Suvarna Nethralaya, our vision is to revolutionize eye care by providing world-class treatments with cutting-edge technology and compassionate patient care. We believe that clear vision is not just a necessity but a fundamental right, and we are committed to making advanced ophthalmic treatments accessible to all. Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art modular operation theaters, offering procedures like Microincision Cataract Surgery (MICS) and Phacoemulsification, ensuring a painless, stitch-free, and quick recovery experience. With a dedicated team of highly skilled surgeons, optometrists, and healthcare professionals, we strive to set new standards in eye care, blending expertise with innovation.

Our commitment goes beyond treatment—we focus on preventive eye care, personalized patient experiences, and affordability through cashless facilities and 0% EMI options. As we move forward, we aim to continually evolve with the latest advancements in ophthalmology, ensuring that every individual receives the best possible care. At Suvarna Nethralaya, we don’t just restore vision—we enhance lives, empowering our patients to see the world in all its beauty and clarity. Your trust drives our excellence, and we are honored to be a part of your journey to better vision.
